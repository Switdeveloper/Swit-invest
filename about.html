OUR STORY

From Humble Beginnings to Digital Leaders

Founded in 2018 by a group of digital marketing enthusiasts, SwitInvest started as a small agency with a big vision: to help businesses succeed online through cutting-edge technology and personalized service.

2018

Founded

SwitInvest was founded with a mission to provide affordable, high-quality digital marketing services to small and medium businesses.

2019

First Major Client

Secured our first enterprise client, helping them increase online revenue by 150% in just 6 months.

2020

Expansion

Expanded our team and service offerings to include SEO, PPC, and social media marketing.

2021

Technology Focus

Began integrating AI and automation tools into our service offerings to provide cutting-edge solutions.

2022

Growth Milestone

Reached 100+ active clients and launched our first proprietary AI chatbot platform.

2023

AI Innovation

Launched our AI Agent Development division, becoming one of the first agencies to offer custom AI agents for businesses.

2024

Industry Recognition

Awarded "Best Digital Marketing Agency" at the Florida Business Awards and expanded nationwide.
